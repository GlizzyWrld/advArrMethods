What is the difference between var and let?

With var you can reassign and redeclare, but with let you cant redeclare with the let keyword. Var allows you to access hoisted variables. Let creates a block scope.

What is the difference between var and const?

With var you can reassign and redeclare and const you cannot do either. With var you can access hoisted variable. Const creates a block scope.

What is the difference between let and const?
 with let you can reassign, but with const you cant redeclare or reassign 

What is hoisting?

Basically it's how javascript compiles things such as variables into specific scopes and by putting variables at the top of scope. With var, it lets you access a variable name and undefined value before its used
